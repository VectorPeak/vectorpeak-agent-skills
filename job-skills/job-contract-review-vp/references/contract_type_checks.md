# Contract Type Checks

Use these checks as prompts for likely missing protections and risk categories. They are not jurisdiction-specific legal rules.

## Lease

- security deposit refundability and deadline
- landlord entry notice
- habitability and repairs
- early termination and penalties
- rent increase or renewal terms
- late fees and caps
- subletting and assignment

## Employment

- non-compete scope, geography, and duration
- IP assignment boundaries, including side projects
- termination and severance
- probation terms
- confidentiality duration
- dispute resolution and fee shifting
- unilateral policy changes

## NDA

- definition of confidential information
- term and survival period
- residual knowledge clauses
- independent development exception
- compelled disclosure
- non-compete disguised as confidentiality
- return or destruction of materials

## Freelance

- payment schedule and acceptance criteria
- kill fee or cancellation fee
- scope creep and change requests
- IP transfer timing
- portfolio rights
- liability cap
- late payment remedies

## SaaS Terms

- data ownership
- unilateral modification
- auto-renewal and cancellation window
- SLA and remedies
- data export and deletion
- liability limitation
- arbitration/class action waiver

## Loan

- interest type and rate changes
- prepayment penalties
- default triggers
- collateral and guarantee scope
- acceleration clause
- fees
- cure period

## Purchase Or Sales

- warranty and returns
- delivery and acceptance
- liability cap
- payment timing
- force majeure
- dispute resolution
- inspection rights
